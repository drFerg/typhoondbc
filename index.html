Hello There

We Are Typhoon

Typhoon is a London based Dragon boat club of 15 years. We're a top team in the UK Premier league and have competed around the world on international trips. We're a fun bunch of people who love to paddle, eat dim sum and travel.

What we get up to...

On and off the boat

Paddling

We paddle hard throughout the year, win races and take home trophies. We are an all season team, paddling through rain, sun, snow or even a typhoon...

Coaching

Whether you want to learn a new skill, shave off a few kilos or go for gold with the GB squad, our coaches will train you to be one of the best!

Travel

We love to paddle at home, but we love to paddle abroad even more. We organise international trips throughout the season. Past trips include China, Hong Kong, Italy, Germany, Malaysia, Spain...

Dim Sum

We love to wind down after a hard paddle at our local dim sum joint, breaking bao and sharing stories.

Social

We believe the key to a fast boat are strong bonds, so we organise regular gatherings off the water to strengthen the team and have celebrated many birthdays and celebrations. This is also how we sneakily recruit our non-dragonboat friends...
