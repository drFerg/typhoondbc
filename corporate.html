Looking for a fun team-building event?

Typhoon has you covered!

Typhoon is a London based Dragon boat club of 15 years. We're a top team in the UK Premier league, with GB coaches and paddlers. We can cater to all shapes and sizes, from one off team-building sessions, training teams for events, to hosting charity regattas.

Dragon boating is the fastest growing participation sport in the UK, encourages participation from all ages and abilities, and is a fantastic spectator sport. Dragon boat crews consist of 16 - 20 paddlers and a drummer, paddling as hard as they can to the beat of the drum, soaring to the line before anyone else.

What we can offer

For a great event

Paddling

Dragon boating is the ultimate team sport, everyone needs to work hard and keep time to propel the team to the finish line. You provide the paddlers and a peppy drummer, we'll steer you to victory.

Coaching

Our team of qualified coaches can create training plans to suit your crew's needs, whether it's for a fun team building session or to take gold at the next event.

Racing

Whether you've got 2 crews or 20, you'll be racing before you know it. Once you get the thrill of paddling head-to-head on the water, you'll never look back.

Catering

A well fed crew is a happy crew! We can provide on-site catering for events of all sizes. Let us know your dietary requirements and everyone will be looked after.

Trophies

We pride ourselves on our bespoke dragon boat trophies, designed in-house by our passionate dragon boaters. We love them so much, they're hard to give away...

Corporate

We love to spread the dragon boat fever, whether it be training corporate or charity teams for team-building, charity regattas, or hosting corporate and charity race days.
